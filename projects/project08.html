Create a logo for a Non-profit organization that promote cultural dialogue between different minorities, including American Indian

Ipê is a tree traditionally found in south America, as a fundamental of the association name, it was important to incorporate it in the logo. The multiple colors used reflect the multicultural aim and font is rounded overlapping parts to put emphasis on multiplicity and integration.
