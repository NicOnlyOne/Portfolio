Ipê – Association pour le dialogue interculturel

The Non-profit organization

Ipê - Association for Intercultural Dialogue: Research and Action is an association created in September 2012 to boost cultural dialogue between different minorities, including American Indian. In a perspective combining fundamental and applied research, the Ipê Association is involved in the cultural, linguistic and educational, organizing youth training workshops and Aboriginal adults, in which the transmission of knowledge and know-how is enhanced by the exchange of knowledge between generations.
